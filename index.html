why did I choose QA?

During my military service in the IDF, after completing a Command course and Master course, I served as head of the “Mars” system by Elbit, in the brigade sector in Ugdat Gaza. As part of my duty, I worked directly with Elbit. My role was to teach others the system, verify its functioning, find faults in software and send them to Elbit.
As a result, I realized that QA interests me and suits my abilities.
I completed my QA studies, and I'm continuing to Automation studies. Now I'm looking forward to my next challenge! I believe that with the extensive knowledge I gained in the course, the abilities and the desire, I will be able to bring added value to the team I will join.
